How the forecast is calculated

Transparent pipeline from historical matches to daily championship odds.

Model pipeline

  1. Ingest `tsl_dataset.csv` with match results from 1958-2020.
  2. Calculate team baselines: win rate, goals, home edge, recent form, big match results.
  3. Blend in current season inputs like market value, managers, and transfer spend.
  4. Simulate the full season with Monte Carlo match outcomes using Poisson scoring.
  5. Aggregate thousands of seasons into points, title odds, Europe, and relegation rates.
