\section{Football Analytics: A Case Study in Applied Intelligence}

\subsection{Technical Foundation}
Our Turkish Süper Lig prediction system demonstrates advanced intelligence methodologies applied to sports analytics, utilizing 62 years of historical data (1958-2020, 18,079 matches) combined with real-time strategic intelligence.

\begin{tcolorbox}[colback=mctgold!10,colframe=mctgold,title=\textbf{Technical Specifications}]
\textbf{Data Sources:} Historical match records, financial reports, transfer databases, managerial records \\
\textbf{Methodology:} Bayesian hierarchical modeling with Monte Carlo simulation \\
\textbf{Validation:} Wilson confidence intervals, backtesting, proper scoring rules \\
\textbf{Output:} Probabilistic forecasts with uncertainty quantification
\end{tcolorbox}
